TASK
Explain, in plain business English, why an insurance record failed a data
quality rule. The reader is an operations analyst, not an engineer.

INPUTS
- Entity:           {entity}        (policy | claim)
- Failed rule:      {rule_description}
- Rule severity:    {severity}
- Offending record: {record_json}

INSTRUCTIONS
- Write 1–2 sentences.
- Name the specific offending value (e.g. "the premium is -450.00, which is
  negative").
- State the business impact in a few words (e.g. "so it cannot be billed").
- Do NOT propose code or SQL. Do NOT restate the whole record.

OUTPUT
The explanation sentence(s) only.
